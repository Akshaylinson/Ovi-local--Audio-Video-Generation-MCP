Prompt Format Guide

Speech Tags

<S>Your speech content here<E>

Text enclosed in these tags will be converted to speech in the generated video.

Audio Description

Audio: YOUR AUDIO DESCRIPTION

Describes the audio or sound effects present in the video (place at end of prompt).
